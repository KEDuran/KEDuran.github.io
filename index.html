Bio

My name is Krystal E. Duran, and I am an aspiring web developer. This portfolio site is my very first website, and despite its basic format, I am very proud of it! Here is a very short bio about me.

I grew up in a small border city named San Benito, Texas. I come from a medium sized family of six. I have three sisters and a loving Mom and Dad. I attended the University of Texas Pan American (now know as the University of Texas Rio Grande Valley) and earned a Bachelor in Political Science, Bachelor in Psychology, and a Master of Public Administration.

I met my wonderful husband, James, when I was 10 years old. We grew up in the same small city and went to school together. When we were both seniors in high school, I asked him to be my date to prom and its all history from there. We have been together for 12 years, and each of those years have been the best!! We currently live in Pflugerville, Texas in our first home that we bought in 2016.

I firmly believe in giving back to community, so it is no surprise I found myself working in both the public and non-profit sector. I know government and advocacy services are not perfect, but I believe even the smallest gesture of kindness or contribution to society matters. I am currently employed as Business Operations Analyst for the Texas Association of School Boards, which is a non-profit organization that advocates for public schools. I come from a long line of educators so working for public school advocates proves the apple does not far fall from the tree.

Outside of working and doing a lot of homework for my bootcamp coding course, I love travelling, playing video games, reading, and doing jigsaw puzzles. Thank you for taking the time to read my brief bio! After I complete the coding bootcamp, I hope to make this portfolio website even better.
